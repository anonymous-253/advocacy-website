10 Steps to Your Advocacy Plan


୨୧ 1. Identify an advocacy challenge or opportunity.

୨୧ 2. Determine the key audiences.

୨୧ 3. Find out what those audiences currently know or perceive.

୨୧ 4. Determine how each audience receives its information.

୨୧ 5. Establish measurable objectives for each audience.

୨୧ 6. Define message points for each audience.

୨୧ 7. Determine the communication activities to deliver those messages.

୨୧ 8. Decide what resources are necessary to complete each activity.

୨୧ 9. Establish a timeline and responsible party for each activity.

୨୧ 10. Evaluate whether you have reached your objectives.
